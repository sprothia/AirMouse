About

This project allows you to use your hand as a virtual mouse, as well as for other functionalites like volume control and system settings. I built the "virtual" mouse using Python, incorporating many libraries & frameworks such as Mediapipe, CV2, autopy, pyautogui, etc.... I used Flask, HTML/CSS to build this website so it's accesible to the end user ‡ Siddharth Prothia ‡

Requirements

Webcam

Have access to an HD webcam and attach it to your computer

Lighting

Make sure your background is not dark and hand is clearly visible

Make sure your hand is centered and straight inside the camera frame.
Press Q to quit on the camera window.

Mouse Hand Gestures

Palm should face camera

1⋅ Mouse

• To move your mouse, keep all your fingers down except the pointer finger, which you will move inside of the purple box.

2⋅ Left-Click

• To left-click, hold up the pointer finger and rapidly open and close your thumb.

3⋅ Scroll-Down

• To scroll down, hold up a fist, with all other fingers down.

4⋅ Scroll-Up

• To scroll up, hold up a 2, with only the pointer and middle finger up.

Other Hand Gestures

Palm should face camera

1⋅ Volume Up

• To increase volume, lower the pointer finger slightly while holding up all other fingers.

2⋅ Volume Down

• To decrease volume, lower the middle finger slightly while holding up all other fingers.
